What Is Lead Generation?

Lead generation is the process of identifying, attracting, and capturing interest in a product or service with the goal of developing a sales pipeline. Itâ€™s a vital component of any marketing strategy and often serves as the first step in turning a potential customer into a loyal one.

A lead is anyone who has shown interest in your offerings. They could be a person who filled out a form, clicked on an ad, signed up for a newsletter, or interacted with your content in some way. The idea is to turn interest into actionâ€”and eventually, into revenue.

Types of Leads: An Overview

Before diving into your services, it's important to understand the different types of leads:

  • Cold Leads: People who have not interacted with your brand before. Requires outreach and nurturing.
  • Warm Leads: Individuals who have shown some interest (clicked ads, visited your website).
  • Hot Leads: Prospects who are ready to buy or are already in your sales funnel.
  • Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs): Interested but not yet ready to buy.
  • Sales Qualified Leads (SQLs): Evaluated by your sales team and considered ready to convert.

B2B Lead Generation

What is B2B Lead Generation?

B2B (Business-to-Business) lead generation involves identifying businesses that might be interested in your product or service. These leads usually represent companies rather than individuals and often require a strategic, value-driven approach.
